About ECDE

The Early Childhood Development Education program in Naivasha focuses on nurturing young children, ensuring they receive proper educational resources, guidance, and support. We monitor children's progress through monthly statistical data capture and engage with parents and teachers to improve learning outcomes.

  • Promote quality early childhood education
  • Ensure accurate data collection and reporting
  • Support teachers and parents in child development
